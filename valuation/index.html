Valuation Drivers

What buyers and brokers weigh when pricing a business.

  • Recurring Revenue

    Maintenance plans and subscription contracts command premium multiples at exit.

  • Customer Retention

    Low churn signals durable cash flow and reduces buyer risk.

  • Growth Rate

    Consistent year-over-year revenue growth supports higher valuation ranges.

  • Profit Margin

    Strong SDE margins relative to industry peers increase buyer confidence.

  • Owner Dependence

    Transferable management and systems reduce key-person discount.
