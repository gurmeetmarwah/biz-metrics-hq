What Drives Margins?

The operational levers that separate top-quartile operators from the rest.

  • Labor Efficiency

    Revenue per employee and utilization rates are the primary margin lever in service businesses.

  • Recurring Revenue

    Predictable contract income reduces marketing costs and stabilizes margins.

  • Route Density

    Clustered stops and tight scheduling minimize drive time and labor waste.

  • Commercial Accounts

    B2B contracts offer volume but often trade margin for stability.

  • Pricing Power

    Specialized skills, licensing, and brand reputation enable premium pricing.
