Revenue Drivers

The five factors that most influence how much a business earns.

  • Employees

    Revenue scales with field staff count — technicians, cleaners, or clinicians drive top-line growth.

  • Service Area

    Geographic density and market size determine account volume and average contract values.

  • Pricing

    Ticket size, hourly rates, and contract pricing directly impact revenue per customer.

  • Recurring Revenue

    Subscription contracts, maintenance plans, and repeat schedules compound annual revenue.

  • Commercial Contracts

    B2B accounts typically generate higher annual values than residential one-off jobs.
